Fight Your Inner Demons


GLADiator is a tool to help you identify and record your emotions. Moods are the heart of GLADiator (and you too) so we developed a system that can accurately analyze audio to identify your current mood.

We'll Help You


Once you record you mood that's where we step in. No matter if your mood is negative or a positive one, we'll offer jokes, inspirational quotes, and even breathing techniques to get you back on track and feeling yourself again!


Live Better
