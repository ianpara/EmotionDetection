Feel better with GLADiator


Life is hard, we understand. With GLADiator you'll be able to vent your feelings without the fear of judgement. GLADiator will record your mood and help you achieve inner peace by suggesting different ways to wind down based on your current mood.


Fight Your Inner Demons


GLADiator is a tool to help you identify and record your emotions. Moods are the heart of GLADiator (and you too) so we developed a system that can accurately analyze audio to identify your current mood.

We'll Help You


Once you record your mood that's where we step in. Depending on if your mood is negative or a positive one, we'll offer jokes and inspirational quotes to get you back on track and feeling yourself again!

Live Better


GLADiator was created to be a guiding hand that helps you learn healthy coping exercises that you can apply to every day life. Our team developed GLADiator to be a form of mobile assistance that can detect emotional episodes in an easy and friendly way.
